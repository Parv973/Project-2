Services

Just try to solve the printer problem from your end by follow these steps otherwise call us on 1800-679-1569.

Not Printing

If your Printer is not printing and does;t show any error, please check weather your printer is connected with usb and if it is WIFI model, ensure you have estabilished connection and device is enabled.

Low-Ink Error

If you are getting blank prints or print in one color, that is sign that you are not having ink. All the new model of printers shows low-ink warning. So you know it’s time to refill the cartridges.

Bad Quality of Printing

Printers are widely in text quality; inkjet uses text suitable for home, school, business use, while most of lasers are capable of printing text for more professional look documents. Please Check print setting before printing.

Paper Jam Issue

While working with any printer it is common that you will face paper jam issue. Follw these Steps :-

  • Turn Off Printer.
  • Open door from where paper are in and out.
  • Carefull take out the paper sheets.
  • close the door of printer and turn on it
  • and please try to print the paper.

Printer Spooler Error

It is a in built software that stores prints jobs on temporarily basis in computer hard disk until the printer is ready to print. This error ocurrs because due to some data may be corrupted then spooler service damage, which lead to issue with printing. you need to clear the the spooler from setting and reinstall the printer.
